Our Story

Raising expectations
for real estate

Founded in 1979 with a single vision — to create the finest spaces that India has ever seen — Veltora Group has grown from a regional developer into a global real estate powerhouse with projects across Mumbai, Pune, Bengaluru, Hyderabad and London.

We combine four decades of experience with the expertise to deliver both quality and scale at a pace that is unmatched in the industry. Every development carries our signature commitment to design excellence, enduring quality, and the art of living well.
